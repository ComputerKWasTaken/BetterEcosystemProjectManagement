Naming Proposal

Conduit

The channel is open.

01 — Analysis

Name Analysis

Conduit — a channel through which something is conveyed. Water through a pipe. Electricity through a cable. Data through a bridge. The word is clean, singular, and carries an inherent sense of flow.

Unlike the other candidates, Conduit is abstract. It does not reference AI Dungeon directly, and it does not reference scripting. It describes what the system is at its most essential: a passage between two sides.

02 — Positioning

Seamless, Invisible Infrastructure

Conduit positions the system as elegant plumbing that disappears when it works. The core message is about seamlessness — data flows between scripts and the extension as naturally as if the boundary did not exist.

Conduit is BetterDungeon’s two-way bridge between AI Dungeon scripts and extension-side capabilities. Scripts publish state, request data, and render UI through Conduit — and BetterDungeon answers. The channel is always open. The protocol is invisible. The result is a script that can do anything the browser can.

The best infrastructure is the kind you forget is there. Conduit is not a product you interact with — it is the reason your script can ask for the weather and get an answer. It is the reason your story can render a health bar that updates every turn.

No setup ceremony. No connection handshake. No mode to activate. The heartbeat signal confirms Conduit is present, and from that point forward, the channel is open.

04 — Audience

Target Audience

  • Primary: Developers and technically-minded creators who appreciate clean abstractions and elegant tooling.
  • Secondary: The broader AI Dungeon community. The name is infrastructure branding — like “WebKit” or “Electron.” Creators know it; players experience the result.
  • Tertiary: Third-party module developers. Conduit sounds professional and platform-grade — important if BetterDungeon opens the module system.

06 — Visual

Design Direction

  • Tone: Clean, minimal, confident — exactly like this document. Architectural diagrams, not fantasy art.
  • Iconography: Two parallel lines with a flowing connection. A simple glyph of two endpoints with a smooth arc. Avoid busy imagery.
  • Color: Cool steel blue or silver on deep charcoal. Metallic but not cold. “Precision engineering.”
  • Typography: Geometric sans-serif with generous letter-spacing. “CONDUIT” in all-caps is clean and balanced.

08 — Summary

Summary

Conduit is the architect’s name. It says: there is a clean, reliable channel between your script and the capabilities it needs, and you do not have to think about how it works. The marketing focus is on seamlessness, elegance, and the invisible quality of good infrastructure. It works best if BetterDungeon positions itself as a professional-grade platform that creators can trust.
