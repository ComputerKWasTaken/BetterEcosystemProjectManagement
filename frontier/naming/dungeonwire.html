Naming Proposal

Dungeonwire

“The line between your story and the world.”
◊ • ◊ • ◊

Name Analysis

Dungeon (AI Dungeon — the game, the world, the creative space) + Wire (a direct line, a live connection, a channel that carries signal).

The name is rooted in the product it serves. It does not abstract away from AI Dungeon — it claims the connection. Dungeonwire is the wire running between your story and the outside world.

◊ • ◊

Marketing Focus

The Live Connection to AI Dungeon

Dungeonwire positions the system as the official communication backbone for AI Dungeon scripting. The core message is about reliability, presence, and always-on connectivity between the game and BetterDungeon’s capabilities.

Dungeonwire is the live wire between AI Dungeon and BetterDungeon. It carries script state, module requests, widget layouts, and AI calls through a persistent two-way channel built on story cards. If BetterDungeon is installed and a scenario uses Dungeonwire, the connection is live.

Think of AI Dungeon as a world. Your scripts live inside that world — they run, they shape the story, they react to player input. But they have always been isolated. They cannot see outside the dungeon walls.

Dungeonwire is the line out.

§
Script Side
The scenario script writes to reserved Dungeonwire story cards
Ω
Extension Side
BetterDungeon watches cards, routes to modules, writes responses back

The wire itself: WebSocket interception, mutation replay, write queue — reliable and invisible.

Target Audience

  • Primary: AI Dungeon community members who identify with the game’s brand and want tools that feel native to the ecosystem.
  • Secondary: Scenario creators evaluating BetterDungeon. “What’s Dungeonwire?” naturally leads to explaining BetterDungeon’s capabilities.
  • Tertiary: Players experiencing Dungeonwire-powered scenarios. The name is evocative enough to spark curiosity.

Visual Direction

  • Tone: Industrial-meets-fantasy — like this very document. A telegraph wire strung through a dungeon corridor.
  • Iconography: A stylized wire connecting two nodes — dungeon side (torch, stone arch) and extension side (gear, circuit). Alternatively: a pulsing signal line with a dungeon aesthetic.
  • Color association: Copper and amber on dark stone. Warm metallic tones that convey reliability and permanence.
  • Typography: Elegant serif like Cinzel for headings, literary body font. “DUNGEONWIRE” reads well as a single compound. Could stylize as “Dungeonwire”.

Assessment

Boons

Product affinityImmediately recognizable as belonging to the AI Dungeon ecosystem.
Memorable compoundA single word that tells a story. People remember and can spell it.
Intuitive metaphorEveryone understands what a wire does — connects things and carries signal.
Community identityAI Dungeon players call their games “dungeons.” A wire into the dungeon resonates.
Searchable“Dungeonwire” returns zero results today. Entirely owned by BetterDungeon.

Banes

AI-Dungeon-specificIf BetterDungeon expands to Voyage or other platforms, “Dungeonwire” becomes awkward. Manageable with sub-branding.
Longer prefixAt 11 characters, card titles like dungeonwire:heartbeat are notably longer. Not a functional problem, but worth noting.
“Wire” connotationOutside the community, could imply a WebSocket library rather than a module runtime. Lead with capabilities.
◊ • ◊

Summary

Dungeonwire is a world-building name. It says: there is now a direct line between your story and everything BetterDungeon can do. The marketing focus is on connection, presence, and the tangible link between the AI Dungeon game world and the extension’s capabilities. The wire is not just infrastructure — it is part of the dungeon.
