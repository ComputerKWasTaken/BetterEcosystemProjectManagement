# Image Prompt Techniques

> Brief guide to writing effective image prompts for better See mode results.

## Overview

The quality of generated images depends heavily on how you write your prompts. Effective prompts give the AI clear guidance about what to create. These techniques help you get better results.

## Basic Prompt Structure

Order prompts from most to least important:

```
[Medium] of [Subject] in [Style] with [Details] by [Artist(s)]
```

The AI prioritizes content at the beginning.

## Key Elements

### Medium

What type of image:
- "a photograph of..."
- "a digital painting of..."
- "an oil painting of..."
- "a pencil sketch of..."
- "a watercolor illustration of..."

### Subject

What's in the image:
- Main focus: "a knight in armor"
- Action: "fighting a dragon"
- Context: "in a burning castle"

### Style

Artistic approach:
- Genre: "fantasy", "sci-fi", "noir"
- Movement: "art nouveau", "impressionist"
- Mood: "dark and foreboding", "bright and cheerful"

### Details

Specific elements:
- "dramatic lighting"
- "highly detailed"
- "intricate armor design"
- "foggy atmosphere"

### Artist References

Mimic specific styles:
- "by Greg Rutkowski"
- "in the style of Studio Ghibli"
- "inspired by Van Gogh"

Combine artists for unique blends.

## Quality Boosters

Terms that often improve results:

- "highly detailed"
- "sharp focus"
- "beautiful"
- "intricate"
- "8k resolution"
- "award-winning"
- "professional"
- "masterpiece"

These are often included in default Image Style.

## Lighting Terms

For photorealistic or dramatic images:

- "dramatic lighting"
- "soft lighting"
- "backlighting"
- "golden hour"
- "studio lighting"
- "cinematic lighting"

## Camera/Perspective

For specific compositions:

- "close-up portrait"
- "wide shot"
- "bird's eye view"
- "low angle"
- "dutch angle"

## Common Mistakes

### Too Vague

- BAD: "a person"
- GOOD: "a young woman with red hair wearing medieval armor"

### Conflicting Terms

- BAD: "bright, dark, colorful, monochrome"
- GOOD: Choose a consistent direction

### Too Much Detail

- The AI can only handle so much
- Prioritize key elements
- Keep prompts focused

## Known Limitations

The AI struggles with:

- **Hands**: Often malformed; try to exclude from frame
- **Text**: Cannot render text accurately
- **Multiple Subjects**: May merge or confuse them
- **Specific Faces**: Won't replicate real people accurately
- **Complex Poses**: May look unnatural

Work around limitations:
- Crop hands out of frame
- Avoid text in prompts
- Focus on single subjects

## Iterative Approach

1. Start with a simple prompt
2. Generate and assess
3. Add details for next attempt
4. Repeat until satisfied

Each generation costs Credits, so refine thoughtfully.

## Example Prompts

### Fantasy Character

```
portrait of an elven mage, silver hair, glowing blue eyes, 
ornate robes, holding a staff, magical aura, fantasy art, 
highly detailed, dramatic lighting
```

### Landscape

```
vast mountain range at sunset, snow-capped peaks, 
dramatic clouds, golden light, digital painting, 
epic scale, by Albert Bierstadt
```

### Action Scene

```
knight battling a dragon, fire and smoke, 
castle ruins, dynamic pose, dark fantasy, 
dramatic lighting, highly detailed
```
